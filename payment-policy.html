Payment Policy

Information about our payment methods and processes

Payment Methods

At Clan India Lifestyle, we offer various payment methods to ensure a convenient shopping experience. You can choose from the following payment options:

Payment Method Description Processing Time
Credit/Debit Cards We accept Visa, MasterCard, and RuPay cards Instant
UPI Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and other UPI apps Instant
Net Banking All major banks supported Instant
Wallets Paytm, PhonePe, Amazon Pay Instant
Cash on Delivery (COD) Pay when you receive your order At delivery

Payment Security

We take payment security very seriously. All payment transactions are processed through secure and encrypted channels. We use Razorpay, a trusted payment gateway, to ensure that your payment information is protected.

We never store your complete credit card information on our servers.

Payment Process

  1. Select your products and add them to your cart
  2. Proceed to checkout
  3. Fill in your shipping details
  4. Choose your preferred payment method
  5. Complete the payment
  6. Receive order confirmation

Credit/Debit Card Payments

When paying with a credit or debit card, you will need to provide the following information:

  • Card number
  • Cardholder name
  • Expiry date
  • CVV (the 3-digit code on the back of your card)

UPI Payments

For UPI payments, you will be redirected to your UPI app or you can scan a QR code to complete the payment.

Billing

After your order is placed, you will receive an email with your invoice. You can also view your order details and download invoices from your account dashboard.

Refunds

In case of a refund, the amount will be credited back to the original payment method used for the purchase. The time taken for the refund to reflect in your account depends on your bank or payment provider:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days
  • UPI/Net Banking: 3-5 business days
  • Wallets: 1-2 business days

For more information about refunds, please refer to our Return & Refund Policy.
